Assignment: Personal Storymap

Brief Introduction

I could tell my story in 1,000 or more words, but it would be a lot easier just to show the places that best capture my essence.

I’ve been to a bunch of different places – even as far across the world as Italy – but there are only seven I have in mind that are true to me. For example:

  • The place I was born is an integral part of my existence. Although I don’t know much about my birth, it’s the place I took my first breath.
  • My elementary and middle school years were critical in my intellectual development, but there are also friends and memories from those places that are embedded in my core.
  • The same can be said about high school and my summer camp, but those places have a more tangible and viewable impact on who I am today.
  • There are other stupid spots that might not mean anything to you, but mean everything to me.

All of these places are a part of who I am and you can see them all on Storymap.
